= Trip Planning Guide

== Introduction

The Trip Planning Guide helps AccessTravel users organize and manage accessible travel itineraries effectively.  
This guide provides step-by-step instructions to save locations, plan trips, and use accessibility information to make informed travel decisions.

== Saving Accessible Locations

1. Use the search bar to find accessible locations.
2. Apply filters according to your accessibility preferences.
3. Click *Save* on any location you want to include in your trip plan.
4. Access saved locations from your dashboard.

== Building a Trip

1. Navigate to *My Trips*.
2. Click *Create New Trip*.
3. Add saved locations in the desired order.
4. Review accessibility details for each location.
5. Save or update your trip as needed.

== Editing or Updating a Trip

* You can reorder locations by dragging them in the trip editor.
* Remove locations by selecting *Remove*.
* Update notes and accessibility observations for each location.
* Changes are automatically saved in your account.

== Sharing Your Trip

* You can share trip plans with other registered users.
* Shared trips respect your accessibility preferences.
* Recipients see all accessibility information you included.

== Tips for Effective Trip Planning

* Double-check accessibility details for transport and accommodations.
* Include contingency options for unexpected barriers.
* Encourage fellow travelers to provide reviews to improve community data.

== Next Steps

After planning trips, you can:

* Contribute accessibility reviews for visited locations
* Explore additional platform features, such as filtering by specific accessibility criteria
* Return to xref:gettingstarted.adoc[Getting Started] if you need a refresher
