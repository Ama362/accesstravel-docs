= Getting Started with AccessTravel

AccessTravel helps travelers with accessibility needs plan and navigate trips across air, sea, and road transportation.
This guide walks you through the basic steps required to start using the platform effectively.

By the end of this guide, you will understand how to create an account, explore accessible travel options, and use accessibility information to make informed travel decisions.

== Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure that you have:

* A stable internet connection
* A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
* A valid email address for account registration

No prior technical knowledge is required.

== Step 1: Create an AccessTravel Account

To get started:

. Visit the AccessTravel platform
. Select *Sign Up*
. Enter your email address and create a secure password
. Confirm your email address using the verification link sent to your inbox

Once verified, you can sign in to your dashboard.

== Step 2: Set Up Your Accessibility Preferences

After logging in for the first time, you will be prompted to configure your accessibility preferences.

These preferences help AccessTravel tailor results to your needs and may include:

* Mobility requirements
* Visual or hearing considerations
* Assistance needs at transport terminals
* Seating and boarding preferences

You can update these settings at any time from your profile.

== Step 3: Explore Accessible Travel Options

Use the search feature to explore accessible:

* Flights
* Cruise journeys
* Bus and ground transportation

Each listing provides accessibility-related details such as:

* Boarding assistance availability
* Facility accessibility
* Seating and space accommodations
* Support services offered by the provider

== Step 4: Review Accessibility Information

AccessTravel aggregates accessibility information from service providers and community contributors.
Review this information carefully to ensure it aligns with your travel needs.

Where available, user-contributed insights provide additional context about real travel experiences.

== Next Steps

Once you are comfortable navigating the platform, you can:

* Save favorite routes and providers
* Contribute accessibility feedback
* Explore advanced filtering options
* Learn how AccessTravel maintains accessibility standards
