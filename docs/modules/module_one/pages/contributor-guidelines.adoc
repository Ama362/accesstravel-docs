= Contributor Guidelines

== Introduction

AccessTravel relies on community contributions to maintain accurate and up-to-date accessibility information.  
This guide explains how users can contribute reviews, photos, and accessibility data responsibly and effectively.

== Who Can Contribute

Any registered user of AccessTravel can contribute, including:

* Travelers who have visited a location
* Accessibility advocates
* Partner service providers
* Admin-approved contributors

== How to Submit a Contribution

1. Navigate to the location page you want to review.
2. Click *Add Review* or *Submit Accessibility Information*.
3. Fill in the structured form with:

   * Accessibility features observed (e.g., wheelchair access, braille signage)
   * Photos documenting accessibility aspects
   * Notes on any barriers or helpful accommodations

4. Submit the contribution for review.

== Content Guidelines

To ensure consistency and clarity, contributors should:

* Use clear, factual language
* Avoid subjective or biased statements
* Follow standard spelling, grammar, and capitalization
* Focus on accessibility-related details
* Respect privacy of individuals and organizations

== Review and Approval

* All contributions are reviewed by Admins before they are publicly visible.
* Contributors may receive feedback to clarify or improve submissions.
* Approved contributions become part of AccessTravelâ€™s verified accessibility database.

== Best Practices

* Take photos that clearly show accessibility features.
* Provide measurements or exact details when possible (e.g., doorway width, ramp slope).
* Update older contributions if accessibility features change.
* Check other user submissions to avoid duplication.

== Reporting Issues

If you notice incorrect or inappropriate content:

* Use the *Report Issue* button on the location page
* Provide details about the problem
* Admins will review and act as necessary

== Next Steps

After contributing:

* Monitor your submissions for feedback from Admins
* Explore xref:trip-planning.adoc[Trip Planning Guide] to use your contributions in real travel planning
* Continue learning about accessibility standards to improve the quality of contributions
